Our Mission

The Quinnipiac Computing Club, or QCC, is the official club for those with a passion to code. We believe that programming is not only fun, but is a necessary part of the world we live in. Our mission is to get as many students as possible excited about the world of computing.

About Us Goals Meetings
Our club began in 2015 when a group of students realized there was no real club for Software Engineers, Computer Scientists, Game Designers, or anyone with an interest in computing. Since then, we have been growing this club to where it is now. At a total of 92 members past and present, we are quickly growing into a larger and larger club. Every semester, we hold events like the Hackathon, which is an all day coding competition with cash prizes. We also run different programming challenges that test for accuracy and efficiency, while also having guest speakers and midterm/final study sessions. If any of this sounds interesting to you, please do no hesitate to become a member! It is free and only takes minutes.

The Quinnipiac Computing Club aims to achieve a certain amount of goals in order to uphold the standards we have placed on this club. It is important that members of the club understand these goals as well, as it is all of our responsibilities to ensure these goals are being met. They are as followed:

  • Spread computing to as many students as possible
  • Create a positive and fun atmosphere where students can get excited about programming
  • Establish a community for fellow developers to share coding knowledge and learn from each other
  • Provide real world applications for programming beyond classwork
If we can achieve on these goals, then the QCC will continue to be a positive club for Quinnipiac!

The Quinnipiac Computing Club typically holds meetings when necessary, but there are monthly meetings that we hold for the duration of the semester. Officers are required to attend, and all members are invited to join us. We discuss upcoming events, potential theme ideas for hackathons, and always order food!
