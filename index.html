Get ready for an electrifying scramble tournament this July 2025 at Whitney Farms Golf Course. One thing’s for sure—golf fans are in for an unforgettable showdown. AJ, Tom, Justin, Brandon, Mark, Matt, Adam, Wick, Eddie, Landon, Spencer, and Mike will form teams and battle it out for bragging rights and glory. In true scramble style, each team member tees off and then collectively selects the best shot to play from. From there, all players hit their next shots from that same spot, continuing the process until the hole is completed. It’s a format that blends individual talent with sharp teamwork and strategy.

As teams navigate the challenges of the course—wherever it may be—they’ll be tested on precision, decision-making, and unity. Whether it’s AJ and Tom’s pinpoint drives, Justin and Brandon’s game-planning, or Eddie and Landon’s clutch putting, each duo brings its own flair to the competition.

Spectators can expect high-energy action, camaraderie, and plenty of memorable moments as the teams go head-to-head. With pressure mounting and every shot counting, only one team will rise above the rest to claim victory.

This year’s scramble promises to deliver excitement, competition, and the true spirit of the game—don’t miss it!

Awards will be given out on the afternoon of July 29th. The following awards will be handed out after the tounrment is complete:

  • Overall Champion: This award would honor the golfer who emerges as the ultimate winner of the tournament, showcasing exceptional skill, strategy, and consistency throughout the event.
  • Best Team: This award would be presented to the team that displays outstanding teamwork, coordination, and synergy throughout the tournament, regardless of their final position.
  • Most Valuable Player (MVP): This award acknowledges the individual golfer who contributes significantly to their team's success, showcasing exceptional skills, leadership, and performance throughout the tournament.
  • Longest Drive: This award would recognize the golfer who hits the longest drive during the tournament, demonstrating exceptional power and precision off the tee.
  • Closest to the Pin: This award celebrates the golfer who hits the shot closest to the pin on a designated par-3 hole, showcasing impressive accuracy and control with their approach shot.
  • Best Putt: This award acknowledges the golfer who sinks the most remarkable or crucial putt of the tournament, demonstrating exceptional touch, reading of the green, and nerve under pressure.
  • Most Improved Player: This award celebrates the golfer who demonstrates the most significant improvement in their game throughout the tournament, showcasing dedication, perseverance, and progress.
